a date with makerhaus

Category : mischievo · No Comments · by mischievo Nov 21st, 2013

I was super excited to find out last week that I had been invited to participate in the very first holiday pop up sale at makerhaus! I had some stock on hand, but I also had a ton of fun cranking out some new products.

One of the new products I will be offering on saturday are wishlets! I plan to donate all proceeds from the sale of all wishlets to the red cross for typhoon relief in the Philippines.

Please swing by makerhaus in fremont this saturday and say hello!

makerhaus
122 northwest 36th street
seattle, wa 98107 

saturday, november 23rd from 10am to 7pm
